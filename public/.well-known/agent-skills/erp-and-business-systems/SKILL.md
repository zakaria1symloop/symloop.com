# ERP, CRM, and Business Systems — Symloop Technology

We deliver custom ERP, CRM, WMS (warehouse management), GMAO/CMMS (industrial maintenance), GED/DMS (document management), and BI/analytics platforms for enterprises in MENA, the Gulf, and Africa.

## Capabilities

- **Custom ERP** — finance, HR, manufacturing, supply chain modules built to client process
- **WMS** — warehouse management with carrier integrations (Yalidine, ZR Express, Maystro, regional players)
- **GMAO / CMMS** — industrial maintenance, IoT-integrated, SCADA-aware
- **GED / DMS** — document management, electronic signature (compliant with Algerian Law 15-04 and equivalents)
- **CRM** — sales, customer service, multi-channel including WhatsApp Business
- **BI and analytics** — Power BI alternatives, custom dashboards, data warehouses
- **Integration** — SAP, Odoo, Microsoft Dynamics, Salesforce, Sage
